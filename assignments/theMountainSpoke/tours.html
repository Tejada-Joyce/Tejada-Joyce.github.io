Adventure Tours

Time to live

Sometimes trying something new can be scary. But, aren't you tired of the same boring vacations? It is time to overcome those fears that don't let you live. And what better way to do that than by taking advantage of the beautiful views of Bozeman, Montana!

Hurry and see which of these tours suits you better, and start living!

Gallagator Trail

  • Days: Monday, Wednesday
  • Length: 2.2 total miles
  • Skill Level Required: Easy
  • Cost: $ 15.00
  • Location: Located in Bozeman, running southwest from South Church to the Museum of the Rockies on Kagy Boulevard.

West Bridger

  • Days: Tuesday, Friday
  • Length: 13.7 total miles
  • Skill Level Required: Easy to moderate
  • Cost: $ 25.00
  • Location: Found on the north edge of Bozeman along the western flank of the Bridger Range.

Heather Lake

  • Days: Thursday, Saturday
  • Length: out and back 9 miles
  • Skill Level Required: Advanced
  • Cost: $ 30.00
  • Location: South of Bozeman down Hyalite Canyon road, about a mile past the waterfall.
