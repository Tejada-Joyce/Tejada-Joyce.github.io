Cycle the Continental Divide !

What is this event about?

The Mountain Spoke challenges a fixed course annually called "Cycle the Continental Divide." It includes a route that is the world's longest off-pavement cycling route. This route is highlighted by long dirt roads and jeep trails that wend their way through forgotten passes of the Continental Divide.

More Information

  • The event starts the 2nd Week of July and finishes the 1st Week of August.
  • Length: 3100 miles
  • Skill Level Required: Advanced
  • Cost: $ 600.00

Basic Equipment

  • Accessories: Glasses, hydration systems, high-power lights and GPS systems.
  • Protective gear: Helmets, body armor and gloves.
